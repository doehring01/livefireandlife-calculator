Privacy Policy

Last updated:

FIRE and Life (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices. By using this site, you agree to the terms below.

Information We Collect

  • Usage data: pages visited, device/browser info, approximate location (via IP), and referral sources (if we use analytics).
  • Cookies: small files stored on your device for things like preferences and analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser.
  • Newsletter data: email address and optional name if you subscribe.
  • Contact submissions: information you send via forms or email.

How We Use Your Information

  • Operate and improve the site and calculators.
  • Send newsletters you opt into (you can unsubscribe anytime).
  • Respond to support or content requests.
  • Analyze aggregate traffic and performance.

Legal Bases (GDPR)

  • Consent (e.g., newsletter signup, optional cookies).
  • Legitimate interests (security, analytics, site improvement).
  • Contract (providing requested content/tools).
  • Legal obligations (complying with law enforcement if required).

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent (e.g., unsubscribe from emails).
  • Request a copy of your data (“data portability”).

To exercise rights, contact us at hello@livefireandlife.com.

Data Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes above unless a longer period is required by law.

Sharing

We don’t sell personal information. We may share limited data with service providers (e.g., email newsletter, analytics) under contracts that protect your data.

International Transfers

We may process data in the U.S. and other countries. We use reasonable safeguards for cross-border transfers where applicable.

Children’s Privacy

This site is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards. No system is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We’ll revise the “Last updated” date and, if changes are material, provide a more prominent notice.

Contact
