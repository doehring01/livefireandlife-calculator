What is FlexFI?

FlexFI is a modern approach to Financial Independence that recognizes life isn’t static. Instead of aiming for a single, rigid savings rate or a one-time goal, FlexFI adapts your plan to the stage of life you’re in—while keeping your long-term trajectory to FI intact.

Why FlexFI?

  • Early career: lower expenses → higher savings rate may make sense.
  • Raising kids / HCOL realities: shift to more take-home pay, still capture employer match and targeted pre-tax savings.
  • Caregiving / health / career pivots: temporarily prioritize flexibility and liquidity.
  • Empty nest / higher income years: accelerate contributions to pull FI forward.

How FlexFI relates to common FI flavors

ApproachCore idea
Lean FIMinimal lifestyle to hit FI at a lower number.
Coast FISave early, then compound to traditional retirement without new contributions.
Barista FIUse part-time work to bridge expenses while investments grow.
FlexFIAdapt across stages: adjust savings, work style, and spending as life evolves.

Put FlexFI into practice

  1. Right-size contributions for this stage: Capture your full employer match, then tune your 401(k)/HSA/IRA based on cash-flow needs.
  2. Model the trade-offs: Use the Take-Home Pay Calculator to see how different contribution levels affect your monthly breathing room.
  3. Revisit quarterly: Life changes—so should your plan. Set a reminder to check your numbers every quarter.
