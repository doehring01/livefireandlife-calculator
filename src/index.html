Meet Ashley

I'm Ashley — working toward Financial Independence while navigating the same trade-offs many of you are: family, career, and a life worth living today. LiveFIREandLife isn't about perfection. It's about building a flexible, realistic plan that moves forward even when life doesn't cooperate. Everything here — the calculators, the blog, the newsletter — is something I actually use. FI doesn't have to mean sacrificing today for a someday that never comes.

📅 Tracking progress since 2020 🎯 CoastFI target: 2030

Why FIREandLife?

We believe FI is about freedom and purpose—not just a number. Life changes, priorities shift, and your plan should flex with them. That's the heart of FlexFI: adapt your savings, investing, and lifestyle to different stages of life while still moving forward.
