<h1>About FIREandLife</h1>

<p><strong>Our Mission:</strong> Bringing clarity to Financial Independence while balancing fulfillment with the time you're given.</p>


<p>Life is more than just saving money. Our philosophy is rooted in these principles:</p>

<ul>

  <li>Life still happens on the path to FI—plan for the unexpected.</li>

  <li>Denying yourself joy for an extended period can harm mental health.</li>

  <li>If you don’t know how to enjoy life now, you won’t magically know how to live after FI.</li>

  <li>Financial Independence is a tool, not the purpose—finding your purpose matters most.</li>

</ul>


<p>We provide tools, strategies, and education to help you build wealth AND a life you love today.</p>
