Projections

This planner runs annual projections in today’s dollars (real returns). Add life events—like a child, a car purchase, or buying a home—and see how they change your savings, spending, and estimated FI date.

Inputs

Sum of all accounts; we model a single blended portfolio for MVP.
Pension, rental net, annuity income, etc. We subtract this from spending.

Life Events

Events can add a one-time hit (down payment, car cash) and/or a recurring change to annual expenses for a number of years (child-related costs, mortgage vs. rent).

Quick Result

Enter inputs and click Run Projection.
