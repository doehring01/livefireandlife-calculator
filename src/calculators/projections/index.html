Projections

This simple planner runs annual projections in today’s dollars (real returns, i.e., after inflation). Contribute before retirement, spend after—see balances by age and whether your portfolio lasts.

Inputs

Sum of all accounts; we model a single blended portfolio for MVP.
Pension, rental net, annuity, etc. Subtracted from spending.
Calculations are real; this only affects the “Nominal” display toggle.
