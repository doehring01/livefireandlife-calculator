FI Contribution Optimization Calculator

This calculator helps you allocate your annual savings across Traditional 401k, Roth 401k, and Brokerage to maximize estimated after-tax wealth at retirement. It models current-year tax savings from pre-tax 401k deferrals, employer match, long-term growth, and dividend tax drag in taxable accounts. Assumptions are simplified and user-editable; this is educational, not tax advice.
