About FIREandLife

We help you pursue Financial Independence (FI) without sacrificing the seasons of life that matter most. That’s FlexFI: an adaptive approach to saving, investing, and spending that changes as life changes, like kids, career shifts, recessions, caretaking, or a well earned break. We created the Investing Order for FI to help people build a simple, step by step foundation before jumping into complex strategies.

FAQ

Do you store my data?

No. The calculators run locally in your browser. Your entries are saved to your device (localStorage) only.

Is this financial advice?

Educational only. We share frameworks and math to inform your choices. Not financial, tax, or legal advice.
