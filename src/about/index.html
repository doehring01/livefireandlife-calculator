About FIRE and Life

FIRE and Life exists to make Financial Independence practical and humane. We build calculators, write scenario-based posts, and share tools that help you move toward FI—without sacrificing the seasons of life that matter most.

What is Financial Independence (FI)?

Financial Independence means your investments and passive income can cover your living expenses, giving you the freedom to choose how you spend your time. Many people start with the “25× annual expenses” rule of thumb (a 4% withdrawal rate) as a quick way to estimate an FI number—but your plan should reflect your life, not just a number.

At FIRE and Life we focus on FlexFI—adapting your plan to life’s seasons while still moving forward: starting a family, living in a high cost-of-living area, caring for parents, changing careers, or simply making space for what matters.

Ready to explore the numbers? Start with the Take-Home Pay Calculator and our FI Formula guide.

Our approach

  • Clarity over complexity: simple tools and clear assumptions.
  • Trade-offs, not dogma: tax optimization vs. real life today.
  • FlexFI mindset: your plan can adapt and still make progress.
