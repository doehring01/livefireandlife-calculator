About FIREandLife

We help you pursue Financial Independence (FI) without sacrificing the seasons of life that matter most. That’s FlexFI: an adaptive approach to saving, investing, and spending that changes as life changes—kids, career shifts, recessions, caretaking, or a well-earned break.

What is FlexFI?

Adaptive Plan

Dial savings up or down as life evolves—without losing the long-term trajectory.

Real-World Math

Cash-flow-first calculators (taxes, healthcare, volatility) that reflect how money actually moves.

Purpose Over Percentages

FI is more than a single withdrawal rate. Plan for the life you want, then fund it intentionally.

Educational only. We share frameworks and math to inform your choices. Not financial, tax, or legal advice.
