Our Mission

Traditional FI advice often locks you into one rigid path: maximize savings, wait it out, and then live. We think there’s a better way. FlexFI is a practical, adaptable approach that lets you keep moving toward FI while adjusting to real life—kids, careers, health, and changing priorities.

The FlexFI Principles

1) Adapt by Season

Dial savings up or down as life changes—without losing sight of the long-term plan.

2) Plan for Reality

Taxes, healthcare, sequence risk, childcare—our tools and posts factor in the messy stuff.

3) Measure What Matters

Track both total net worth and FI-eligible assets so your number actually reflects spending power.

4) Simple > Perfect

Clear calculators and scenarios you can use today beat spreadsheets you’ll never open.
