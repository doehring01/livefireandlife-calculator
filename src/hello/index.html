Welcome to FIREandLife

Thanks for stopping by! You might’ve scanned a QR code or been referred here directly.

This site is for anyone on the FI journey—whether you’re just getting started or you’ve mastered the basics and want to dive deeper. You’ll find tools, calculators, and real-world scenarios that help you move forward without losing sight of life today.

My whole angle is simple: FI isn’t just one number or one rule. It’s about designing a life you actually want, then funding it intentionally. That’s why I talk a lot about FlexFI—an adaptive way to pursue FI that bends with the seasons of life.
