Welcome to FIREandLife

Thanks for stopping by! You may have scanned a QR code or been referred here by me directly.

This site is built for people on all points of their Financial Independence (FI) journey. Whether you’re just getting started and trying to figure out the basics, or you’ve already mastered the fundamentals and want to dive deeper into strategy, you’ll find tools, stories, and insights to help along the way.

My goal with FIREandLife is simple: to show that pursuing FI isn’t just about a single number or rule. It’s about designing a life you love while still making smart financial progress. That’s where concepts like FlexFI come in—balancing saving for tomorrow with living fully today.
